You take cover behind a concrete road barrier. You peek over your cover to scan the scene. One group is dressed in dirty, white hazmat suits. You ask yourself, “are they wearing garbage bags?” Yet, you realize their organized look and numbers. “I count maybe eight people.” The other group has about five people, and are clothed in regular, worn out clothing. “They look pretty confident. But is that enough?”

You stop peeking for a second just to listen. A muffled voice yells, "We’re trying to help you! This world will rebuild itself. You people help the start of it all. Please!"

"I appreciate the ‘please’ but screw you," yells a woman, "Put down your damn weapons and let’s have a proper conversation.”

“You aimed at us first!”

“What do you expect?!”

The voices are building up more aggressively. Their weapons look ready to fire.
