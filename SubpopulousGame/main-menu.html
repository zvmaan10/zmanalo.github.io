After a mysterious force had struck the earth in 2020, humanity has come near to its age of extinction. People were becoming delirious and highly aggressive. Certain people would act irrational and violent, thus harming and ending the lives of others. These people wereare infected. This virus attacks victims neurologically and physically. Ultimately, the infected seemingly die on their own. It has been said they “disappear.” For some reason, bodies of the infected are rarely recovered. Some like to believe they just bury themselves a hole. Others claim the infected drown themselves in the ocean. However, nothing is certain.

This world is left uncertain. It’s suspected that the cause of infection is a great exposure to an unearthly gas. The origin of this gas was never traced back to. How it spreads from person to person is a mystery as well.

Thirty years have passed, and humanity has barely rebuilt itself. The current population still remains unknown, but there are people out there. The government as well as the military have fallen, and communities have built themselves up from what remains. A certain trend is that these communities are built away from what used to be very populated areas. Today, people have an innate fear of former “major cities.” Those who enter the cities never come back.
